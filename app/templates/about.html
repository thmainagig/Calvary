History

The Institution was established under the objective to provide wholistic courses in the guidance of Biblical guidance. Dr. Karanja together with a team of dedicated professionals, founded Calvary Vocational Institute in 2005. Over the years, we have expanded our programs to include a wide range of vocational skills, that empower individuals to excel in their careers while upholding Christian values.

Our Mission

To equip learners with practical skills, professional competence, and godly values, empowering them to transform their communities, excel in their careers, and serve with excellence in every sphere of life.

Our Vision

To be a leading vocational institute recognized for excellence in skills training, character development, and community transformation through Christ-centered education.
